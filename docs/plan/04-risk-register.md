# 04 — Risk Register

> Living register expanded from Research §10 plus risks surfaced during planning. Review cadence: **monthly** at the architecture review. Owners are role names per [`08-team-and-raci.md`](./08-team-and-raci.md).

> **Scope narrowed 2026-04-21 per [ADR 0022](../adr/0022-oss-single-tenant-framework-scope.md).** Enterprise-specific rows deleted or narrowed: the anchor-enterprise-customer row is gone; SSO edge-case risk narrowed to a single OIDC-adapter risk; multi-tenant data-leakage row is gone; Stripe / marketplace rows are gone. New rows R-23–R-27 added for self-host-first operational surface and LMS conformance cadence. Operational posture follows [ADR 0021](../adr/0021-self-host-first-infrastructure-principle.md).

## Categories

- **tech** — technical implementation risk.
- **market** — adoption, competition, customer-demand risk.
- **security** — vulnerabilities, threat-model risk, data integrity.
- **dependency** — third-party library, platform, vendor risk.
- **people** — bus-factor, hiring, burnout risk.
- **legal** — license, GDPR, compliance risk.
- **ops** — self-host operational risk (backup, restore, upgrade cadence) — added 2026-04-21 per [ADR 0021](../adr/0021-self-host-first-infrastructure-principle.md).
- **external** — risk carried by a third party whose behavior we do not control (e.g. LMS regressions) — added 2026-04-21 per [ADR 0022](../adr/0022-oss-single-tenant-framework-scope.md).

## Legend

- **Likelihood:** L (low, <25%), M (medium, 25–60%), H (high, >60%) — within the current phase window.
- **Impact:** L (annoying, one-week delay), M (notable, one-month delay or quality-gate miss), H (material, a phase slips or a Critical-severity incident).
- **Status:** open, monitoring, mitigating, resolved, accepted.
- **Review cadence:** how often the risk is re-scored.
